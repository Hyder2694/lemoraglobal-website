Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Lemora Global LLC ("Lemora Global," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you interact with us, including through our website, phone, email, and text messaging (SMS/MMS) communications.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information from candidates, clients, and website visitors:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Resume, work history, and professional qualifications
  • Information you submit through our website forms or provide during phone, email, or text communications

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Match candidates with relevant job opportunities
  • Communicate with candidates and clients regarding job opportunities, interviews, scheduling, and related recruitment activities
  • Provide staffing and workforce solutions to our business clients
  • Improve our services and respond to inquiries

SMS/Text Messaging Communications

By providing your phone number to Lemora Global, you may receive calls and text messages from us regarding job opportunities, interview scheduling, and related recruitment communications. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP, or get help by replying HELP.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories of consent or use of mobile information are excluded from this disclosure and exempted from this requirement.

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information, including phone numbers, to third parties or affiliates for marketing or lead generation purposes.

How We May Share Information

As a staffing agency, we may share a candidate's resume and relevant qualifications with the specific employer or client we are working with on their behalf, for the purpose of matching candidates to job opportunities. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your Choices

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may also contact us directly to request that we update, correct, or delete your personal information.
